My pitch involves combining Illustration and Coding to create interactive stories that focus on the black woman and her identity. These stories will be hosted on an individual website which can be accessed from any device with wifi. Hair, Colourism and gender expression are some of the topics I plan to delve into in the future. I aspire for this website to be a safe space for black women to visit when they are feeling alone or misunderstood.
